Q: You are an expert chess player. You are given a chess board with FEN format. Your goal is to choose a better move given two candidate moves. The FEN of the given chess board is "2r2k2/p4r2/2p5/1p2Q3/4p3/PP1P1b2/2K5/8 w - - 0 38". Which move is better?
A: 7. Qf4. If 7... Qf5+ 8. Kg6! and White checks back Black's king, Qf5+ 9. Kg7 wins instantly!
